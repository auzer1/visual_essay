What is it?

Pixel art is a form of digital art, created through the use of software, where images are edited on the pixel level.
These are the important things that pixel art have not to include.
Display resolution: when display resolution increase, the amounts of works also increased. For example, in low resolution, it is really hard to find what is what, however, in high resolution, number of pixels will be increase and is able to make more easy to make it.
Palette swap: To build the different colours of characters, people use fixed colours from pallet such not make all the pixels again. The easiest way to find pixel art is what is not.
Gradients: select two colours and mix it.
Blur tool: make colour transparent to background.
anti-alias: make different colour of pixels smooth. So, pixel art is not include these three.
